About CareCore Hearing

Learn more about our mission to make hearing health accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

At CareCore Hearing, we believe that everyone should have access to basic hearing health tools. Our digital hearing tests provide convenient, at-home screening to help you monitor your auditory health between professional exams.

While our tests are not a replacement for professional hearing care, they can help identify potential issues early and encourage regular hearing monitoring.

How It Works

Our tests use the Web Speech API to generate speech samples at different volumes and frequencies. Each test includes calibration instructions to help ensure accurate results.

Your test results are stored securely in your personal dashboard, allowing you to track changes over time and share them with your audiologist if needed.

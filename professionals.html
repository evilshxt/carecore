Stay Healthy With These Tips

Stay Active Daily

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity every day.

Benefits Include:

  • • Improved cardiovascular health
  • • Enhanced muscle strength
  • • Better mental wellbeing
  • • Reduced risk of chronic diseases

Eat Nutritious Foods

Incorporate plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

Focus On:

  • • Colorful vegetables and fruits
  • • Whole grains and legumes
  • • Healthy fats like avocados
  • • Lean proteins and plant proteins

Prioritize Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to help your body recover.

Sleep Tips:

  • • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • • Limit screen time before bed
  • • Keep your bedroom cool and dark
